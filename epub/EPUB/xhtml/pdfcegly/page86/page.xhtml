Compositions

 

A composition is a text where sentences and words are put together to make a meaningful

 

written work.

 

There are four kinds of compositions: descriptive, narrative, expository, and comparative.

 

* Descriptive compositions are the ones that describe or enlist features of something or someone.

 

* Narrative compositions are the ones that tell a story. It can be about one or more events told

 

in sequence.
