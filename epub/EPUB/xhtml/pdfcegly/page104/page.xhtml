Book Report

 

Book reports are similar to book reviews. However, book reports focus mainly in giving a summary

 

of the book rather than talking about the value of the book and opinion.

 

Some of the elements of a book report are:

 

1.
Title, author, publisher, year of publication

 

2.
Setting of the story where and when it takes place (present, past or future)

 

3.
Plot of the book

 

4.
Description and names of the characters

 

5.
Summary of the plot

 

6.
Opinion or point of view

 

7.
Examples and quotes from the book to support your point of view
